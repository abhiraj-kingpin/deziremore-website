About Us

Our story, our passion

At Dezire More, we believe that ethnic fashion is not just clothing — it is a celebration of identity, heritage, and the timeless spirit of Indian culture. Founded with a deep love for craftsmanship and elegance, we curate handpicked collections that blend traditional silhouettes with a contemporary sensibility. Every piece in our store is thoughtfully sourced to ensure quality, beauty, and wearability — from the everyday kurta to the grandest bridal lehenga. We are committed to making premium ethnic fashion accessible to every modern Indian woman, regardless of occasion or budget. Our team works closely with skilled artisans and weavers to bring you designs that honour tradition while keeping you effortlessly in style. When you shop with us, you are not just buying an outfit — you are carrying a legacy forward with grace.
